Bill Nye is the number one science guy. Bill is a popular science influencer who has his own science show oriented for children. As a child, I watched a multitude of Bill's films in my science courses. To this day I am a huge Bill Nye Fan

I attribute much of my science knowledge to Bill Nye The Science Guy!
