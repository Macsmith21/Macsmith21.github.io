Food

There is so much food that has become "pop culture". Many of these are more so staples of cultural cuisines but there is also more representation than that, while Chicken Tikka Masala is the most popular indian dish and the Cheeseburger is an American staple, single dishes have also been associated with so characters, shows, and places. Some examples could include Homer Simpson and the Donut or the Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles and Pizza. Food is a huge aspect of all culture it only makes sense that it takes its own light in pop culture as well. there are other parts of food that have been considered "pop culture" as well like starbucks as the stereotypical coffee shop all girls go to or certain fads/lifestyles like the "gymbro diet" circulating around beef, rice, and protein shakes.

Places

So many places can be attributed to pop culture. Real or fake many spaces are synonymous with certain lifestyles or narratives. New York City is the classic american big city for sitcoms and Middle-earth is the baseline for a fantasy realm. Even Hogwarts or Gotham are places of pop culture. Even more though there are places that have been constructed with the sole goal of encapsulating an element of pop culture, such as disneyland being the quintessential experience for anyone who is a fan of disney.

Architecture

Architecture is a more subtle aspect of pop culture that most people forget about, yet it is omnipresent through history. Many eras and places can be defined by their architecture. Art-deco, modern, advant-garde, japanese contemporary and much more. All of these design style both inside and out influence the publics tastes, choices, and trends. Many times people use architecture to express themselves. But it is not only architecture as an art but architecture as an atmosphere. The big majestic towers of Hogwarts or the futurisitic landscapes of Tron legacy. Many people associate these architectural designs with their own favorite pieces of pop culture and the art becomes synonymous with the identity of the culture.
