My Top 5

Random Access Memories

Random Access Memories By Daft Punk is an electronically produced album that cannot be defined in a single genre. Released in 2013 as the French duos final album, RAM featured a variety of renound artists and earned itslef 5 Grammy Awards in 2014. I have trouble picking favorites off of this album as I appreciate every track, yet I find myself always returning to the Giorgio By Moroder for its amazing drums.

The Getaway

The Getaway by The Red Hot Chili Peppers is their 2016 press and a difficult pick as my favorite album of theirs. I feel as though I resonate with this album mainly because I grew up being a huge fan of the band and was a teenager when the album released. Therefore, I was extremely hyped and gave it many listens. even though John Frusciante isn't the guitarist on the album, I still find myself enjoying the Guitar as much as I do on other albums
